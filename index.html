Hello, and Welcome to The Safe

Your friend, Pablo, is the owner of a very expensive painting. However, he has forgotten the combination to his safe.
Luckily, you offer your safe-cracking services to him, for the price of 25% of the painting inside the safe.
Pablo is auctioning this painting in 3 days. Will you be able to crack the safe before time runs out?
If not, Pablo's auction will be ruined and you will not be rewarded.
Good Luck!
